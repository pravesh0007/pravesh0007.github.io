About Me

I would describe my education till now as a desire to understand things and constant search to find answers to scientific questions. I completed my Master's in chemistry from the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. During my master's project, I have worked on the synthesis and proton transfer of a triazole derivative. This work was recently published in Spectrochimica Acta, Part A: Molecular and Biomolecular Spectroscopy (https://doi.org/10.1016/j.saa.2022.120911).

I have worked on organic synthesis and electronic spectroscopy (UV-Visible, steady-state and time resolved fluorescence spectroscopy). As proton transfer is an ultrafast process. I got really interested to pursue my Ph.D. in ultrafast processes. Currently, I am working in the laboratory of Dr. Sachin kumar B, IIT Delhi on a short-term project.
